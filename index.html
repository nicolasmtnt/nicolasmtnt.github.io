About me

Hello! My name is Nicolas Martinet, I am Research Fellow at CNRS, working on generative AI applications for haptic feedback under the supervision of Claudio Pacchierotti. Previously, I was a visiting predoctoral fellow at Northwestern University, focusing on Reinforcement Learning for multi-agent autonomous systems and patrolling tasks under the supervision of Prof Qi Zhu.

I hold an MSc in Data Science and a BS in Computer Science. My interests also extend to Mathematics and Quantum Sciences, particularly Quantum Computing and Information.
